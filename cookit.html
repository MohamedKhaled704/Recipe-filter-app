Lentil Bolognese

Rich and robust, this plant-based Lentil Bolognese is hearty, “meaty, ” full of depth of flavor. Toss it with your favorite pasta, or spoon it over creamy polenta– either way, this simple, nourishing vegan meal is one the whole family will enjoy and will quickly become one of your go-to vegan meals!

Avocado Alfredo

This dish tastes like the classic alfredo dish, but with the addition of creamy avocado instead of butter. Just blend ripe avocados with heavy cream, garlic, and plenty of grated pecorino cheese then toss with al dente fettuccine. The result is a rich, uniquely green alfredo ready in just about 25 minutes and sure to delight pasta lovers and avocado lovers alike.
